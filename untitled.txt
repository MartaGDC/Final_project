ANALISIS POSIBLES
1. Date:
Analyze the distribution of queer experiences over time.
Explore trends or patterns in submissions across months or years.

2. Location:
Geospatial analysis of queer experiences across different regions.
Identify clusters or hotspots of submissions on the map.

3. Comments:
Sentiment analysis on comments to understand the emotional tone.
Identify common themes or topics mentioned in the comments.

4. Relationship Between Date and Location:
Explore whether there are patterns or trends in submissions based on both date and location.

5. Length of Comments:
Analyze the length of comments to understand the level of detail or emotional expression.

6. Time Series Analysis:
Investigate if there are recurring patterns or changes in the frequency of submissions over time.

7. Geospatial Clustering:
Use clustering algorithms to identify geographic areas with a high concentration of submissions.

8. Network Analysis:
Explore relationships between different locations, users, or themes mentioned in the comments.

9. Demographic Analysis (if available):
If demographic information is provided, explore relationships based on gender, age, or other demographics.

10. Top Keywords or Topics:
Extract and analyze the most common keywords or topics mentioned in the comments.
